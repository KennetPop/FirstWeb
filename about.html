About Us

Toledo Organic Teas is a unique and inspiring business that was founded in 2020 with a clear vision: to provide exceptional, fully organic teas while empowering the indigenous Ketchi Maya women in San Pedro Columbia Village, located in the picturesque Toledo District of Belize, Central America.

At Toledo Organic Teas, we take great pride in offering a wide range of meticulously crafted teas that are not only delicious and refreshing but also promote a healthier lifestyle and a sustainable environment. All our teas are carefully sourced and produced using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality and purity.

What sets us apart is our commitment to organic farming practices. We firmly believe that nature knows best, and by cultivating our teas in harmony with the environment, we can preserve the rich biodiversity of our region while delivering exceptional flavors and health benefits to our customers. We do not use any harmful chemicals or pesticides, allowing the true essence of our teas to shine through in every sip.

Our business is deeply rooted in the local community. Our two owners, both indigenous Ketchi Mayan women, bring a wealth of cultural heritage and traditional knowledge to every aspect of our tea production. They have dedicated themselves to preserving their ancestral wisdom and promoting sustainable livelihoods for the local community. By supporting Toledo Organic Teas, you are directly contributing to the empowerment of these remarkable women and the preservation of their vibrant culture.

When you choose Toledo Organic Teas, you embark on a journey of taste, wellness, and cultural appreciation. Each sip of our teas transports you to the lush rainforests and pristine landscapes of Toledo District, where the flavors are rich, and the traditions run deep. From our invigorating Ginger Tea to the aromatic Turmeric Tea and the delightful Cacao Tea, our exquisite selection of teas caters to every palate and preference.

But our mission extends beyond the boundaries of taste. We strive to educate and raise awareness about the benefits of organic tea and the importance of supporting sustainable and ethical businesses. We actively collaborate with local farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and providing them with a stable source of income. Together, we are nurturing a community-driven movement that celebrates the beauty of nature and honors the profound connection between people and the environment.

By choosing Toledo Organic Teas, you are not only enriching your tea-drinking experience but also joining a passionate community that values tradition, sustainability, and cultural diversity. We invite you to embark on this remarkable journey with us, explore the flavors of Toledo, and support a business that makes a positive impact on the lives of indigenous women and the environment.
